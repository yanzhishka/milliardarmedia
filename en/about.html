About

Less noise. More meaning.

Milliardar is a journalism agency. We turn facts, conversations and video into clear stories without the noise: fast, careful and with a human voice.

Newsroom

Five people on the editorial flight

Everyone in the newsroom has their own route: facts, filming, editing and delivery.

01 Editor-in-chief, sound engineer Konstantin Sazonov Bio

Birthday: October 2. Konstantin owns Milliardar's editorial line: he picks the topics, gathers the facts and brings stories to a clear, precise form. His focus is the structure of an issue, source-checking and the agency's overall tone — so a story rests on facts yet stays alive and human.

02 Editor-in-chief Atemiy Prokhorenko Bio

Birthday: September 15. Atemiy works with editorial logic and the final assembly of stories: he sharpens the wording, checks the facts and helps separate a strong angle from the noise. His focus is the argument, order in the text and the calm confidence with which a story reaches the reader.

03 Telegram channel host, reporter, camera Mikhail Nazarov Bio

Birthday: June 3. Mikhail works with the agency's visual language: filming, light, composition and the rhythm of the frame. He helps stories speak not only through text but through an image that holds attention and sets the right pace for an issue.

04 Camera and host Anna Dryazgova Bio

Birthday: October 7. Anna runs Milliardar's Telegram direction: she follows the topics, prepares short releases and helps turn the newsroom's observations into clear posts quickly. In reports she handles the lively delivery, the contact with people and the attention to detail a story is built from.

05 Lead developer Lev Lezhenin Bio

Lev owns the technical side of Milliardar: the website, the infrastructure and the newsroom's tools. His focus is stability, speed and the platform the stories run on.
